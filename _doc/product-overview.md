# Forest Ghost 100 Challenge

## Product
A mobile-friendly challenge tracking app for the **Forest Ghost 100 Challenge** — a structured outdoor program where participants complete 100 units (runs, hikes, or sessions) in nature. The app gives each participant a "ghost" — a virtual pacer to chase — making the solo journey feel competitive and alive.

## Users
Outdoor enthusiasts and challenge participants who have signed up for (or want to run) a structured 100-unit nature challenge. They want motivation, accountability, and a way to see their progress visually over time.

## Positioning
An immersive, gamified challenge companion that turns a personal outdoor goal into a competitive narrative. Not a generic fitness tracker — purpose-built for the Forest Ghost 100 program.

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: Forest Ghost 100 Challenge
- **Mood**: Atmospheric, adventurous, grounded in nature
- **Tone**: Motivating and direct — like a trail-running coach who believes you'll finish
- **Palette**: Deep forest greens, earthy tones, misty whites

## Core Capability (MVP)
- Log a completed challenge unit (a "run" or "session")
- Track cumulative progress toward 100 units
- Race your ghost — see your pace vs. a virtual benchmark pacer
- Milestone celebrations at key waypoints (25, 50, 75, 100)
- Live progress dashboard showing sessions completed, ghost gap, and days remaining

## Strategic Principles
- One number matters: completions toward 100
- The ghost creates urgency without social pressure
- Every interaction should feel like stepping onto a trail — fast, intentional, rewarding

## Status
Early concept — core detail (exact unit type, user volume, pricing) to be confirmed through onboarding.
