✕ The Problem

Challengers quit before the finish line.

A 100-unit goal stretches weeks or months. Without a dedicated tracker and something to compete against, participants lose momentum, miss sessions, and abandon the challenge halfway.

✓ The Solution

A ghost that keeps you honest.

Forest Ghost 100 gives every participant a virtual pacer — a "ghost" — to race against. Log each session, watch your score climb toward 100, and hit milestone celebrations that reward every waypoint on the trail.

"Not competing with others. Competing with your ghost."
